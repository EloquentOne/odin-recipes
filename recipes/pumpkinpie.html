Pumpkin Pie recipe

Description

Thanksgiving wouldn't be complete without a homemade pumpkin pie. But we're going to let you in on a little secret: Making the seasonal dessert is actually quite simple. Don't let the season pass you by without trying this easy pumpkin pie recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • 16 oz. can of pumpkin puree
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin pie spice

Steps

  1. Put pie crust in 9 in pie tin
  2. Mix ingredients until smooth
  3. Bake in a preheated oven at 425 degrees for 15 minutes, then reduce temperature to 350 degrees, baking until pie is set.
